Beta Release(0.1.0) - March 3rd, 2017

  • v0.1.0 is the first release of Miracle Me. It will be run in Beta mode for 20 users to identify performance, experience and functionality issues and rectify. The release includes features such as employee search and news feed.

    • Features

    • Single Sign On with Hubble – Utilize your existing credentials to login to the app experience.
    • Employee Search Capability – Native Feature Integration for adding to contacts and placing calls/emails from app.
    • Ability to change background picture for profile – 14 image options available today.
    • News Feed Option to view latest notifications from Operations Team – Send functionality provided to Operations Admins.
    • Issue Management feature for users to log facility issues and check resolution status and comments – Operations Admins will have option to resolve and update issues.
